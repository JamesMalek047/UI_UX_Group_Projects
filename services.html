General Dentistry

General dentistry forms the foundation of lifelong oral health. At NovaSmile, our general dentistry services are designed to prevent, diagnose, and treat a wide range of dental concerns, helping you maintain a healthy, confident smile at every stage of life. From routine cleanings and exams to fillings, crowns, and gum care, our team focuses on proactive, personalized treatment.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics focuses on correcting the alignment of teeth and jaws to improve both function and appearance. At NovaSmile, we offer a range of modern orthodontic solutions tailored to patients of all ages — from traditional braces to discreet clear aligners. Our goal is to help you achieve a straight, healthy smile with treatment plans that prioritize comfort, efficiency, and aesthetics.

Service Price Range (CAD)
Initial Orthodontic Consultation $100 – $150
Traditional Metal Braces $3,000 – $6,000
Clear Ceramic Braces $4,000 – $7,000
Clear Aligners (e.g., Invisalign) $4,500 – $8,000
Retainers (post-treatment) $200 – $500
Monthly Adjustment Visits $80 – $120

Periodontics

Periodontics is the specialized branch of dentistry focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of gum disease and other conditions affecting the supporting structures of the teeth. At NovaSmile, we provide comprehensive periodontal care aimed at preserving your natural teeth and promoting long-term oral health.

Service Price Range (CAD)
Periodontal Evaluation $120 – $180
Scaling & Root Planing (Deep Cleaning) $200 – $400 per quadrant
Gum Grafting $800 – $1,200 per site
Periodontal Maintenance Visit $150 – $250
Laser Gum Therapy $400 – $700
